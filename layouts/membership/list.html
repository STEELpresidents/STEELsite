Membership Options

General Membership

Become a general member of STEEL and join our ranks of future engineers and leaders. Become a member of STEEL and join our community. A General Membership includes access to STEEL meetings, events, and workshops.

Dues: $35 / year

Apply Now

Build Team Membership

Join a build team to take part in a hands-on project and gain valuable technicle and team experience. Teams will contain ~15 members and have budgets of $750. Build Team Memberships are available in addition to General Membership.

Additional Dues: $50 fall, $75 spring

Committee Membership

Committees allow members to contribute directly to the success of STEEL by bettering our organization and community. Each committee plays a vital role in shaping the organization:

  • Internal Affairs Committee – Helps plan and organize STEEL’s social and fundraising events, along with managing STEEL's public image.
  • External Affairs Committee – Focuses on external relations and professional development, as well as creating/managing merchandise.

The ability to join a committee is included with General Membership, with no additional dues required.
