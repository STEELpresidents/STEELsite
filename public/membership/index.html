Membership Options

General Membership

Become a general member of STEEL and join our community of future engineers and leaders. General membership includes access to events, networking opportunities, and voting rights in the organization.

Dues: $35 / semester

Apply Now

Build Team Membership

Take your involvement to the next level by joining one of our build teams. Build Team members work on hands-on projects, gain technical skills, and collaborate closely with peers. This membership is available in addition to General Membership.

Additional Dues: $125 / semester

Committee Membership

Committees allow members to contribute directly to the success of STEEL outside of project work. Each committee plays a vital role in shaping the organization:

  • Events Committee – Helps plan and organize STEEL’s social, professional, and outreach events.
  • Outreach Committee – Focuses on recruitment, community involvement, and spreading awareness of STEEL.

Joining a committee is included with General Membership, with no additional dues required.
